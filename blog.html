Today, I listened to some lectures and took away a few valuable insights:

21.02.2025

Be memorable. If you're driving and see an ordinary cow, you won’t think much of it. But if you see a purple cow, you'll remember it and tell a friend. The same goes for business-stand out to stay in people's minds.

- Your environment shapes you. The worst place to be is in a group where people are neither highly motivated nor completely lazy. It keeps you stagnant.

- Focus beats multitasking. It’s better to concentrate on one thing and become an expert rather than run three businesses in different industries and not excel in any of them.

- Scale matters. Small businesses struggle with every sale, while bigger companies make their entire monthly revenue in a single day.

- Analyze and improve. After every 10 attempts, review what worked and what didn’t, and make the next 10 even better.

Learning is essential, but applying what you learn is what truly makes the difference!
